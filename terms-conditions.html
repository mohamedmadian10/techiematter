Services

The services of the TechieMatter platform are designed to serve as a link between candidates who search for vacancies and employers, as well as assisting employers who want to evaluate and assess candidates through TechieMatter experts. Also, TechieMatter offers many services on the website as follows:

Recruitment:

TechieMatter selects Candidates by filtering their CVs/Reports and presenting them to employers, including but not limited to:
- Selection of Candidates with great care in accordance with the requirements of the job offered by the Employers.
- Send the candidates' CVs/Reports to employers to determine whether the Candidate is suitable for the job or not.

Assessment:

TechieMatter assesses the selected candidates by Employers including but not limited to:
- Assess Candidates through many professional standards, considering the Candidate's past practical and scientific areas of expertise, and issue assessment results to the employers.
- Send the results to the Employers regarding all details of the previous experience of each Candidate.

Expert Interview:

TechieMatter conducts personal interviews with Candidates through TechieMatter Experts, including but not limited to:
- Experts identify the Candidates' previous experience.
- TechieMatter then nominates the Candidates to Employers.

Onboarding:

TechieMatter prepares certain paperwork on recruitment requirements for Candidates in order to facilitate procedures for Employers including but not limited to: legal, administrative and financial papers for Candidates and all necessary for their onboarding process.
